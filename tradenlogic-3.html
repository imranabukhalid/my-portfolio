About Us

Who We Are

Tradenlogic is a full-service environmental solutions company built on responsibility, precision and sustainable practice. We operate across waste management, material recovery, used asset trading and structural demolition — transforming what others discard into resource, and every cleared site into a foundation for what comes next.

From routine commercial waste contracts and bulk scrap metal trading to large-scale used equipment acquisitions and controlled multi-building demolition, our certified team delivers the same standard of excellence across every project — on time, within budget and in full regulatory compliance.

Headquartered in Dubai, UAE, we serve clients across the Middle East, Asia and beyond with an unwavering commitment to transparency, safety and measurable environmental impact on every project we undertake.
